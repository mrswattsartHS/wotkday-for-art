🚀 Tips for Staying on Pace

⏱️ Time Management:

  • • Break big projects into smaller tasks
  • • Set mini-deadlines for yourself
  • • Use the focus timer above
  • • Work on something every class period

📈 Progress Tracking:

  • • Take photos of your work in progress
  • • Keep notes about what you accomplished
  • • Celebrate small wins along the way
  • • Ask for feedback regularly

💡 Stay Motivated:

  • • Remember your artistic goals
  • • Don't aim for perfection, aim for progress
  • • Try new techniques to stay engaged
  • • Share your work with classmates

📌 Work Day Reminders

✅ Before You Start:

  • • Focus on your current assignments
  • • Gather all your art supplies
  • • Set up your workspace
  • • Review what you need to complete

🎯 During Your Session:

  • • Stay focused on your current project
  • • Take breaks when needed
  • • Document your progress
  • • Ask questions if you get stuck

🎨 Remember: Every artist was first an amateur

Focus on progress, not perfection. You've got this!
